European Medical Device Catalogue Ontology (EMDCO): Description back to ToC

Ontology modelling the European catalogue of medical devices and in vitro diagnostics under MDR (EU 2017/745) and IVDR (EU 2017/746) regulatory frameworks, including manufacturers, risks, incidents and field safety corrective actions registered in EUDAMED.
